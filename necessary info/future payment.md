Managing Online Payments LegallySince you are integrating an online payment gateway (like Razorpay or Cashfree) but not storing the final PDF on your server, you must ensure the system remains reliable.The Webhook Trigger: When a client pays, the payment gateway sends a secure network request (webhook) to a Supabase Edge Function.The Database Update: The Edge Function instantly changes the status of that invoice ID from unpaid to paid in the database.Real-Time Sync: Because Supabase offers real-time listeners, the CA's dashboard will instantly show the invoice as "Paid" without reloading the page.On-Demand Receipt: When the CA opens the app later, they can click "View" on that paid invoice. The app pulls the transaction details from the database and instantly previews or downloads the completed receipt PDF on demand.
